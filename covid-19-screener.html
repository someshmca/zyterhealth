Manage symptoms at home

Your answers indicate that you do not have any symptoms or other factors that currently suggest the need for COVID-19 testing.

Symptoms of COVID-19 can include new:

  • Fever
  • Cough or sore throat
  • Shortness of breath or trouble breathing
  • Chills or repeated shaking with chills
  • Muscle aches
  • Nausea, vomiting or diarrhea
  • Loss of smell or taste, or a change in taste
  • Headache

Contact your health care provider for advice if you get new symptoms or if you have close contact with someone with lab-confirmed COVID-19. Close contact means living with that person, being within 6 feet of that person for more than 5 minutes or being exposed to their sneezing or coughing while not both wearing masks. Close contact also means touching their mucus, saliva or shared items while not wearing gloves.

For more information, see Coronavirus disease: What is it and how can I protect myself?
